About Event

DATALIZE is a captivating single day event organized by Data Science Club, VIT Bhopal , for students across any domain irrespective of their branch, will be a great opportunity for students to learn about data science and to connect with experts in the field. The seminars and hands-on workshops will provide valuable knowledge and experience, and the quiz and prize at the end will add an element of competition and motivation for the attendees. Participants will not be charged for this training. All participants will be provided with digital certificates approved from the Data Science club.
